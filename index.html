Zheng Zhao

I am a third year PhD student with UKRI Centre for Doctoral Training in NLP at the University of Edinburgh, working with Shay Cohen and Bonnie Webber. I am affiliated with ILCC in the School of Informatics. I am also a member of the Cohort and EdinburghNLP group.

My research topic is on analyzing and interpreting neural networks for NLP. I am also interested in summarization, discourse, and their related topics.
