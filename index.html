Ashok Urlana

Currently, I am working as a Researcher at TCS research. Earlier, I did my Masters in NLP at International Institute of Information Technology-Hyderabad, under the supervision of Manish Shrivastava. Previously I worked with Chakravarthi Jada

My research topic is on text summarization for Indian languages. I am also interested in bio-inspired algorithms and its related topics.
